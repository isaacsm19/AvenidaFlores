About Us

Avenida Flores

The most luxurious apartment complex in Atenas
Atenas is a small town, known for having The best climate in the world.
Atenas is located between the capital, the airport and the Pacific Ocean with a warm climate surrounded by mountains, hot during the day and cool at night, with an elevation of 698 m/ 2277 feet above sea level.
Atenas has several supermarkets, banks, excellent restaurants, commercial stores, colleges, schools, universities and medical centers.

Atenas, Costa Rica

Atenas has a bus terminal with direct access to:
San José capital (40kms)
Juan Santa Maria International Airport (25kms)
Also, Atenas is close to beaches, you can take buses that pass through the town giving access to them, the most popular:
Puntaleona Beach (56 kms)
Jaco Beach (70kms)
